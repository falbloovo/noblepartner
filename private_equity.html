Overview

At Noble Partners, we take great pride in our team, our track record, and our capabilities. We invest in, and partner with industry-leading organizations and ventures poised for significant growth and unlocking hidden value in order to deliver superior returns to our investors. Moreover, we are disciplined investors, focusing on long-term business fundamentals as well as lasting community benefit and positive impact.

We work thoroughly with our portfolio companies to implement growth strategy and prepare them in advance of eventual divestment, which is the mindset spread across the team and built into the investment cases of our acquired companies from the start.

We make sure that liquidity is made available at the portfolio companies, which is our approach to ensure that each portfolio company is given time to develop and grow before becoming a suitable candidate for exit.

Diversification

We invest across multiple countries, sectors and different investments, in order to build a well-diversified portfolio of investments. Our regional expertise and outlook empower us to evaluate a broad range of opportunities and to select those that have high potential and meet our investment criteria in order to generate the most attractive risk-adjusted returns

Robust Investment Process

We utilize our experience and resources, an efficient, systematic and thorough investment process which has been developed and employed by the team. We follow a thorough investment process involving detailed market research, business and promoter background checks, and extensive financial and legal due diligence. The final outcome of the due diligence process is documented in a report and presented to the Investment Committee, where they collectively make a call on every single investment. The Investment Committee also gets engaged in formulating strategies and value creation for enhancing the value of and/or exiting from such investment.
